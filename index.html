Welcome to Brosely Camera Club's Website



We are a friendly bunch of like minded people, who get together and discuss as many aspects of photography as we can. We get together only once a month, having a break in August and December. The meetings are very informal and easy to follow.

We have workshops and presentations that suit all abilities, from beginner to advanced, phone camera to DSLR. There really is something for everyone. For more please read through our 'About Page' for more about our club.

Please feel free to browse through our galleries and snippets from our members newsletters. Take a look at what we are up to this year in our Programme and/or contact us to find out more.
